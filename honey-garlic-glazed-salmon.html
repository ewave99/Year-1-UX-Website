Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (150g each)
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Sesame seeds and chopped parsley for garnish

Serves: 4

Prep time: 10 mins

Cooking time: 15 mins

Methods

  1. In a small bowl, mix honey, soy sauce, lemon juice, and minced garlic to make the glaze.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Place salmon fillets skin-side down and cook for 4-5 minutes.
  3. Flip the salmon and pour the glaze over it. Cook for another 3-4 minutes, basting the fillets with the glaze.
  4. Remove from heat once the salmon is fully cooked and flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Garnish with sesame seeds and parsley before serving.
