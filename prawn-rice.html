Garlic Prawn Rice

Prep time: 5 mins

Cooking time: 15-18 mins

Serves: 4

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 350g rice
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 400g prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced, plus extra wedges/slices to serve
  • 25g parsley leaves, finely chopped

Method

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions and set aside. Meanwhile, heat 3 tbsp olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  2. Add prawns and cook until pink and cooked through (about 2-3 mins per side).
  3. Squeeze lemon juice over prawns, sprinkle with lemon zest and half the parsley; stir to combine.
  4. Add cooked rice and remaining olive oil to the pan; toss to coat. Cook for 1 minute, then season. Remove from heat, top with remaining parsley, and serve with extra lemon wedges.
