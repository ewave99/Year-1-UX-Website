Flourless Brownies

Prep time: 20 mins

Cooking time: 30 mins

Serves: 16

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g butter, chopped, plus extra for greasing
  • 250g dark chocolate (at least 70% cocoa solids), broken into small pieces
  • 225g caster sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g ground almonds
  • Pinch of salt

Method

  1. Heat oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4. Grease and line a 4cm-deep, 20 x 20cm square cake tin with baking parchment.
  2. Melt 200g butter and chocolate in a heatproof bowl set over a pan of simmering water (ensure the bowl doesn't touch the water), stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and cool for 10 mins.
  3. Whisk sugar, eggs, and salt until pale and foamy (2-3 mins with electric whisk, 5-8 mins with balloon whisk).
  4. Pour in the cooled chocolate mixture and almonds; fold until combined. Pour into the tin, scatter remaining chocolate on top, and bake for 25 mins until cooked with a slight wobble.
  5. Cool for 10 mins, then serve warm or cool completely, cut into squares, and store in an airtight container (keeps for up to 5 days).
