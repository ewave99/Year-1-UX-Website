About

We are Food for Thought. We aim to bring you balanced, feel-good food for a student budget.

Many students arrive at university without prior knowledge or experience of cooking.
Furthermore, the importance of budgeting brings worries of how a varied, interesting diet can be achieved.

At Food for Thought, we're here to help.

We're not obsessed with salads or special dieting plans. We simply know that a varied diet improves your health, and improving your health makes you feel good, and feeling good is extremely important. Especially if you have a busy student schedule. So join us!
