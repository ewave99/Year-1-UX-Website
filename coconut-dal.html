Coconut Dal

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • A small piece of ginger
  • A green chilli
  • A red onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 200g dried red lentils
  • 1 can (400ml) of coconut milk
  • 400ml vegetable stock
  • 1 lime
  • Fresh coriander

Serves: 4

Prep time: 15 mins

Cooking time: 1 hr

Method

  1. Peel and chop the garlic, ginger and red onion. Finely chop the green chilli.
  2. Add the garlic, ginger, red onion and chilli to a large pan with oil. Fry on medium heat until soft.
  3. Add the cumin seeds, coriander seeds, turmeric and cinnamon. Cook until you can smell the spices.
  4. Add the lentils, coconut milk and vegetable stock.
  5. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Turn the heat down to low and cook for half an hour.
  7. Add the juice from the lime and season to taste.
  8. Serve garnished with chopped coriander.
