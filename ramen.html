Ramen Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 250g middle bacon rashers, chopped
  • 8 eggs
  • 5 x 72g wholegrain 2-minute noodles, discard flavour sachet
  • 4 cups (1L) salt-reduced chicken stock, warmed
  • 3/4 cup reduced-fat cooking cream
  • ½ cup finely grated parmesan cheese, plus extra to serve
  • ¼ cup finely chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley

Serves: 4

Prep time: 10 mins

Cooking time: 10 mins

Method

  1. Heat olive oil in a large frying pan over high heat. Cook the bacon, stirring for 2-3 minutes or until crisp.
  2. In a separate pan, bring water to a boil. Gently crack 4 eggs into the water, cover, and cook for 2-3 minutes until whites are set and yolks remain runny. Use a slotted spoon to transfer eggs to a plate.
  3. Break noodles into a microwave-safe bowl, pour over hot stock, and microwave for 2 minutes until cooked.
  4. Separate the remaining 4 eggs, placing the yolks in a bowl. Whisk in cream, cheese, and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add cooked noodles and some broth to the pan with the bacon. Stir in the cream mixture and cook over low heat, tossing until warmed through and noodles are coated, adding more broth if necessary.
  6. Serve the carbonara topped with a poached egg, and sprinkle with parsley and extra cheese.
