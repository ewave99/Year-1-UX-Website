French Bread Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf French bread
  • 4 tbsp butter, melted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 ladleful marinara or pizza sauce
  • 2 handfuls shredded mozzarella
  • 10 pepperoni slices
  • 2 tbsp freshly grated Parmesan
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • Freshly chopped basil, for topping

Serves: 4

Prep time: 15 mins

Cooking time: 30 mins

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C. Slice bread in half lengthwise. Mix melted butter and garlic together and brush all over cut side of bread. Bake for 10 minutes to lightly toast.
  2. Spread marinara on top and top with mozzarella, pepperoni slices, parmesan, and red pepper flakes. Bake again until cheese is melted, (around 10 minutes).
  3. Top with basil and serve.
