Garlicky Bean Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 stick of celery
  • 2 leeks
  • 1 onion
  • Half a bulb of garlic, or around 6 garlic cloves
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Bay leaf
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary or 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1 tsp dried tarragon
  • 2 cans cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 litres vegetable stock
  • 6 leaves of cavolo nero
  • Grated cheese, to serve
  • Chopped parsley

Serves: 6-8

Prep time: 10 mins

Cooking time: 45 mins

Methods

  1. Finely slice the celery, leeks, onion and garlic. Remove the stems from the cavolo nero leaves.
  2. Heat some oil and butter on medium heat. Add the celery, leeks, onion and garlic. Add the bay leaf, rosemary and tarragon. Cook until the vegetables are soft and translucent.
  3. Add the cannellini beans and the stock. Bring to a simmer and cook for half an hour.
  4. Add the cavolo nero and simmer for another 5 minutes, until it is just wilted.
  5. Season to taste.
  6. Serve with grated cheese and chopped parsley alongside buttered bread. The soup may be frozen. Reheat thoroughly before consuming.
